Privacy Notice

At A Forscutt Design we take your privacy very seriously and we do our best to adhere to Data Protection principals and comply with GDPR (General Data Protection Regulations).

Cookies

Cookies are small text files which can store website preferences on your computer, so they can be remembered when you return to web pages. A Forscutt Design website does NOT use cookies as there is no need to store anything locally to your computer or device.

Data Owner

A Forscutt Design is operated by Andy Forscutt as 'A Forscutt Design'.

Under UK GDPR, it is the legal responsibility of website owners and operators to make sure that personal data is collected and processed lawfully. A Forscutt Design will always ensure this is the case.

Data Processors

By using our contact form your personal details will be stored by our web hosting service Netlify and we will receive an email notification containing the personal details to our business email address which is ran by Google.

We will never pass your personal details to any other third parties than those named above for the necessary processing of your contact form request.

Data Retention

We will delete any contact information from at most one year after your latest enquiry or correspondence in compliance with GDPR, but we aim to delete information 21 days after contact.
